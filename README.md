# Executive-HR-Attrition-Insights-and-Retention-Strategy_TABLEAU-Project

## Overview

Analyzed HR data of 1,470 employees to identify key drivers of attrition.
Overall attrition rate: 16.12% (237 exits).

## Key Findings

Higher attrition in Sales and R&D

Employees aged 25–34 showed higher exit trends

Lower job satisfaction linked to higher attrition

Senior roles showed greater stability

## Business Value

Helps HR identify high-risk segments and plan targeted retention strategies to reduce hiring and training costs.
